STRUCTURE

Boys Team Event:

1. 5 Match Format. Singles-Singles-Doubles-Singles-Doubles.
2. Best of 5 amongst the above matches.
3. Team should consist of minimum 5 and maximum 6 players.
4. Only One Player may repeat across the tie sheet.
5. The repeating player will only play ONE Singles and One Doubles.

Girls Team Event:

1. 3 Match Format. Singles Doubles Singles.
2. Best of 3 amongst these matches.
3. Team should consist of a minimum of 3 & maximum of 4 players.
4. Repeating player will play only ONE Single and One Double
5. Maximum one player will be repeated
