RULES

Main Rules



All UG and PG students are allowed (Age should not be more than 25 and no complete PG students team allowed maximum PG students in team are restricted to 3)
1. The duration of Match shall be of 30 minutes, divided equally into 2 halves of 10 minutes each, with a 5-minute interval between the two halves for eliminators.
2. Qualifiers will be played in two halves of 15minutes each and final will be of 2 halves of 20minutes each
3. Standard kabaddi Scoring System will be followed.
4. Time Out: Each team is allowed to take 1 TIME OUT of 90 seconds per half.
5. Referee’s decision is final.
6. In case of tie, an extra time of 5 minutes will be awarded to decide the result of match.
7. Only one team allowed from one institute.
