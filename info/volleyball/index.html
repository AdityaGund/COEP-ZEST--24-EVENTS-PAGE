RULES

Main Rules



1. Rules for the game are according to recent standardized rules by FIVB.
2. Points in a set or No. of sets may vary according to organizers (considering time constraint).
3. Team should consist of all engineering course students from the same institute.
4. Team should consist of maximum 12 players.
5. Only one team allowed from one institute.
6. Team can have maximum 3 players from PG.
7. A complete team of PG students is not allowed.
8. All teams are expected to report 30 min before the match start time.
9. 3 calls will be made by the officials before the start of the match.
10. If a team fails to report before the final call, the team will be disqualified.
11. In case of disputes final authority will lie with the organizing team.
12. All rights are reserved to the organizing team.
